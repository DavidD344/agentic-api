Daniel Sadoc Menasché received his MSc and PhD degrees in Computer Science from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst, in 2011. He received a BS cum laude degree in Computer Science and MSc degree in Computer and Systems Engineering from the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ) Brazil, in 2002 and 2005, respectively. He did internships at INRIA Sophia Antipolis, University of Avignon and Technicolor. In 2011, he joined the Department of Computer Science at the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ), Brazil. His main interests are in the modeling and analysis of computer systems, including computer networks, and smart grids. (Texto informado pelo autor)
