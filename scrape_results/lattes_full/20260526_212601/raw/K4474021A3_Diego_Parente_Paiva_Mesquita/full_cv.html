Since 2022, I am an assistant professor at FGV EMAp. I am broadly interested in fundamental machine learning (ML) research, with a special focus on probabilistic methods and geometric deep learning. Over the years, I have worked on an array of topics like deep generative models, graph neural networks, federated learning, Gaussian processes, physics-informed machine learning. and approximate inference. My work has been published in some of the most prestigious venues in machine learning (e.g., NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, AISTATS, and UAI). I am currently a member of the Brazilian computer society's special committee on AI. (Texto informado pelo autor)
