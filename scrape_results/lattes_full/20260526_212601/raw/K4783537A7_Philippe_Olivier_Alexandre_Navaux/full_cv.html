Harnessing Cloud Computing to Power Up HPC Applications
Descrição: In this research project, our main goal is to explore opportunities for symbiotic, coordinated use of HPC and cloud computing infrastructures. The idea is, starting from an HPC infrastructure such as the ones in place in BRICS member countries, to identify cloud computing aspects that could be explored to provide a far-reaching, flexible (e.g., in terms of adaptation to fluctuating demands), secure and efficient environment for running HPC. We will focus on devising models, algorithms and mechanisms to help users in having access to a suitable execution platform (built on top of open source-based developments) for running their applications, in those scenarios in which users? own infrastructure is missing or lacks required resources. As an integral axis of the project, we will also investigate how different big data, science-related applications can be modeled so as to perform adequately when executed on top of the proposed hybrid environment...

Paralelismo, portabilidade e eficiência de aplicações sísmicas de larga escala em arquiteturas Exaflop - UFRGS
Descrição: Investigar o impacto das arquiteturas de computadores propostas para máquinas da classe de Exaflop nos algoritmos e na codificação de aplicações sísmicas de larga escala. Qual é o consumo energético destas arquiteturas e como melhorá-lo nos níveis de hardware e de software. Descrição: Energy consumption and reliability of multi-core ICs are becoming major causes of concern spanning from data centers and supercomputers to embedded systems. The greater numbers of transistors are used to increase the number of cores and the amount of on-chip memory, and to integrate graphic and other hardware accelerators.
