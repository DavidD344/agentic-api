Alba Cristina Magalhães Alves de Melo

Alba Cristina Magalhaes Alves de Melo obtained her PhD degree in Computer Science from the Institut National Polytechnique de Grenoble (INPG), France, in 1996. In 2008, she was a PostDoc Researcher at the University of Ottawa, Canada; in 2011 she was invited as a Guest Scientist at the Université Paris-Sud, France; in 2013 she did a sabbatical at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya, Spain and in 2018 she was invited as a Guest Scientist at the Institut National Polytechnique de Grenoble, France. Since 1997, she is with the Department of Computer Science of the University of Brasilia (UnB) where she is now Full Professor and Head of the Department. Prof. Melo is Member of the Council of the Brazilian Computer Society (2021-2025), Vice-chair of the IEEE Technical Committee in Parallel Processing and Member of the Brazilian Delegation for the BRICS Working Group on High Performance Computing and Information and Communication Technologies (BRICS WG on HPC-ICT). Her research interests include High Performance Computing, Accelerators (GPU, FPGA), Bioinformatics and Clouds. One of the PhD Thesis advised by Prof. Melo was awarded the Best PhD Thesis in Computer Science in Brazil (Premio Capes de Tese) in 2016. She is also recipient of the 2023 IEEE TCPP Outstanding Service and Contributions Award and the 2019 Wilkes Award for the Best Paper published at The Computer Journal in 2018. Prof. Melo is a Senior Member of the IEEE Society and the IEEE Computer Society. She was Associate Editor of IEEE Transactions on Parallel and Distributed Systems (2015-2019) and she is currently Associate Editor of IEEE Transactions on Computers, Journal of Parallel and Distributed Computing, Future Generations Computing Systems and ACM Computing Surveys. Prof. Melo was co-General Chair of IEEE IPDPS 2024. She served as Program Chair at HiPEAC 2025, co-Program Chair at IEEE/ACM CCGrid 2025, co-Program Chair in IEEE IPDPS 2019 and IEEE SBAC-PAD 2017. In addition, she served as co-Track Chair in several prestigious conferences such as Euro-Par 2025, IEEE Cluster 2023, Supercomputing (SC) 2022, IEEE IPDPS 2022, Euro-Par 2020.Prof. Melo was keynote speaker at prestigious conferences (Euro-Par 2021, SBAC-PAD 2023 and CARLA 2020) and in several international workshops. She coordinated international collaboration projects with Spain and Canada and participates/participated in several international projects with the European Commission, France and Germany, among others.
